
## What You Should Back Up (Before Reset / Downgrade)

Backing up **everything important** is critical before resetting, downgrading, or flashing firmware. Some data **cannot be recovered** once lost.
>While most of this is common sense, not everyone is familiar with the full backup process. If you are unsure, consider watching a YouTube tutorial on backing up your data to a PC or spare phone before proceeding.

----------

### Media & Personal Files

-  Photos and videos (DCIM,Pictures Folder)
    
    -   Camera
        
    -   Screenshots
        
    -   Screen recordings
        
    -   Downloads
        
-   Documents
    
    -   PDFs, Word/Excel files
        
    -   ZIP/RAR archives
        
-   Audio files
    
    -   Music
        
    -   Voice recordings
        
-   Files stored in:
    
    -   Secure Folder
        
    -   Hidden directories
        
    -   App-specific folders (e.g., WhatsApp, Telegram, game data)
        

> **Recommended:**  
> Copy important files manually to a PC, external drive, or cloud storage as a secondary backup.

----------

####  Games

-   Confirm game progress is **cloud-saved**
    
-   Link games to Google, Facebook, or developer accounts
    

> Some apps and games **do not support cloud saves** and will lose progress after reset. (cracked games)

----------

###  Contacts, Messages & Call Logs

-   Contacts
    
    -   Sync to Google or Samsung account
        
-   SMS/MMS messages
    
-   Call history (if needed)
    

> Smart Switch or third-party backup apps can help preserve messages and logs.

----------

### SIM, eSIM & Network Information

-   **eSIM profiles** (often deleted during reset)
    
-   Carrier configuration and APN settings
    
-   Voicemail access numbers and PINs
    

> Contact your carrier if you are unsure how to reissue or restore your eSIM.

----------

### System & Device Settings (Often Forgotten)

-   Wi-Fi networks and saved passwords
    
-   Bluetooth device pairings
    
-   Home screen layout and app folders
    
-   System preferences and accessibility settings
    
-   Wallpaper and lock screen settings
    

----------

###  App-Specific Backups (Manual)

Some apps require **manual backups**, such as:

-   WhatsApp (local + Google Drive backup)
    
-   Telegram (check cloud sync)
    
-   Note-taking apps (export notes if needed)
    
-   Password managers (export vaults securely)
